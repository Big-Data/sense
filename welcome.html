Here are a couple of words to get you oriented quickly

Sense's screen is split into two panes. The left white pane is where you can type requests to send to Elasticsearch. The right black pane is where you see responses.

Sense understands requests in a compact format, similar to cURL:

While typing a request, Sense will make suggestions which you can than accept by hitting Enter/Tab. These suggestions are made based on the request structure and your indices and types.

A couple of quick tips, while I have your attention

  • Submit requests to ES using the green triangle button.
  • Use the wrench menu for other useful things.
  • You can paste requests in cURL format and they will be translated to the Sense syntax.
  • You can resize the editor and output panes by dragging the separator between them.
  • Do study the keyboard shortcuts under the Help button. Good stuff in there.
